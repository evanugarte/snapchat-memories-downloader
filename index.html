How snapchat memories are downloaded to your computer
After uploading your file, the data in the file is read within your browser. The data is then used to download all of your memory files to your computer. By running the entire process within your browser, your snapchat data is not stored anywhere on snapchat-memory-saver.com's servers and stays private to you.
